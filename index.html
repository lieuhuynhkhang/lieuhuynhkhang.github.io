Our group consists of 4 members: Khang, Quang, Kiên and Minh. All of us are quite new to IT and this is our first course related to IT. Our personal information is as follows: Next is Lieu Huynh Khang, ID number s3695455. Khang majors in International business, as such, the Introduction to Information Technology is his elective. Despite that, Khang still has a lot of interest in IT and is thoroughly enjoying what he is learning so far. Growing up, Khang has a hard time finding his true calling since he feels a sense of involvement in everything he does. This is Khang’s third year in RMIT and he has changed majors 2 times. With that being said, there are things that he is more passionate about and has been pursuing for years. Those being basketball, cooking and video games, with video games is his oldest passion. When Khang was a little kid, 5 - years old - his family was only making ends meet. Therefore, one day when his parents decided to get a PC set, Khang was enthralled in this box of magic. Although he's too young to know how to use a computer, he would sit all day and watch his brother play classics like pokemon, digimon, mario, wario, metroids and the endless golden jewels of the era on the gameboy emulator. There's also stick fight games, street fighters and others on the neo geo emulator and many more. This flame never faded through the years, evolving with time, as he learned real-time strategies like warcraft, open world sandbox like minecraft, then mobas like dota and league, to battle royale first person shooters like PUBG. To this day, he still finds it absolutely amazing how a whole world of fantastical exploration and thrilling action could be put in just one rectangular metal box. . This passion peaked Khang’s interest in IT as he tried to learn how these games are created. As for education, Khang did well as he always managed to get in one of the best schools in the city. Especially, Khang excels in English. He is the best student in his highschool english team and managed to get into RMIT without Unistart. Khang’s said that this comes from years of experience playing english games, reading their storylines and watching foreign TV channels.
